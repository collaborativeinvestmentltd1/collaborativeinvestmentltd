About Collaborative Investment Ltd

Advancing enterprise development through strategic management, structured investments, and sustainable growth solutions.

Our Story

Collaborative Investment Ltd (CIL) was founded with a clear mission—to build, manage, and scale sustainable businesses across Nigeria's most promising sectors. The company was created to close a major gap in the SME space: poor management, poor structure, and lack of strategic oversight.

Today, CIL operates as a structured business development and investment company, managing diversified projects and providing enterprise support systems that ensure stability, growth, and long-term profitability.

Our focus remains the same — empowering entrepreneurs, securing investors' interests, and building businesses that stand the test of time.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become one of Nigeria's most trusted business development and investment management companies—building sustainable enterprises that create economic growth and community impact.

Our Mission

To empower local businesses by providing structured management, strategic investments, professional oversight, and long-term development solutions that guarantee sustainable growth.
