Company Overview

Collaborative Investment Ltd (CIL) is a Lagos-based business development and investment management company created to identify, restructure, manage, and scale small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across Nigeria.

Our Mission

CIL aims to improve and empower local businesses by providing strategic investment, management support, and financial partnerships that ensure long-term growth and sustainability.

Our Vision

To be one of Nigeria's most trusted business development and investment firms by transforming small businesses into profitable, stable, and scalable ventures.

Our Values

Integrity, Growth, Collaboration, and Accountability guide all our operations and partnerships.
