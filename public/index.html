Building Nigeria's Business Future

Collaborative Investment Ltd (CIL) is a Lagos-based business development and investment management company dedicated to transforming SMEs into profitable, scalable enterprises across Nigeria.

Our Mission

To empower local businesses through strategic investment, management support, and financial partnerships that ensure long-term growth and sustainability.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted business development firm by transforming small businesses into profitable, stable, and scalable ventures.

Our Values

Integrity, Growth, Collaboration, and Accountability guide every decision and partnership we undertake.
