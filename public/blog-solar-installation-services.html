Executive Summary

Solar installation services represent a highly profitable business opportunity in Nigeria, with the potential to generate 25-35% annual returns through a combination of installation profits and recurring maintenance revenue. With 85 million Nigerians lacking reliable electricity and growing solar adoption, professional installation services are in high demand across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Key Investment Highlights:
  • ROI Potential: 25-35% annually
  • Market Size: $10 billion solar market by 2030
  • Recurring Revenue: 40% from maintenance contracts
  • Growth Rate: 60% annual market expansion
  • Scalability: Rapid expansion across Nigeria's 36 states

1. Massive Energy Access Gap

With only 40% of Nigerians having reliable electricity access, solar power has become the primary alternative energy source for households, businesses, and industries across the country.

2. Growing Solar Adoption

Solar system installations are growing at 60% annually as costs decrease and awareness increases. However, professional installation services remain scarce, creating service gaps.

3. Recurring Revenue Potential

Beyond installation profits, maintenance contracts, system upgrades, and battery replacements create stable recurring revenue streams with high customer retention rates.
